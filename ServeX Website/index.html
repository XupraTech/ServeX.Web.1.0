Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about ServeX

Registration is simple and free. Here's what you need to do:

  1. Enter your name, email and phone number
  2. Create a secure password
  3. Verify your account via email or SMS
  4. Complete your profile with required details

We accept various secure payment methods:

  • Visa cards
  • Mastercard
  • American Express
  • PayPal

All payments are processed securely with SSL encryption.

At ServeX, your safety is our priority:

  • Identity verification for all service providers
  • Background checks and reference verification
  • Transparent rating and review system
  • Data protection with advanced encryption
  • 24/7 support to report any issues

Our pricing system is transparent:

  • Providers set their rates based on market standards
  • You see the estimated price before booking
  • Secure payment held until job completion
  • No hidden fees - what you see is what you pay

ServeX charges a small service fee to ensure a secure experience.

Our cancellation policy is fair:

  • Cancellation 24+ hours in advance: Full refund
  • Cancellation within 24 hours: Charge applied per provider's policy
  • Cancellation by provider: We help find a replacement or full refund

All cancellations are managed through the platform.
